Frequently Asked Questions

What fabrics are your thobes made from?

Our thobes are crafted from high-quality natural fabrics like cotton, linen, and viscose that are lightweight, breathable, and ideal for hot climates.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Orders are usually processed within 3-5 business days and shipped via express international delivery. Custom tailoring may take 1-2 weeks additional time.

Do you offer exchanges if the size doesn't fit?

Absolutely, we want customers to have the perfect fit. Unworn thobes can be exchanged free of charge within 30 days of delivery.

Can I request custom embroidery or detail designs?

Yes! We offer full customization options from thread colors to embroidery patterns. Contact us to discuss your unique design ideas.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unworn merchandise within 30 days of delivery. Refunds are provided minus original shipping fees.
